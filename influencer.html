About Us

In a world driven by digital influence and online perception, building authentic connections with your audience is more important than ever. At TwicTech, we design powerful influencer collaborations and PR campaigns that help brands rise above the noise — gaining visibility, credibility, and consumer trust where it matters most.


As a hybrid of a brand marketing agency and an IT solutions company, we offer a data-driven and creative approach to influencer and PR marketing — ensuring every campaign is not just seen, but felt, shared, and remembered.

The Power of Influencer Engagement and Public Relation

Your customers trust people, not just ads. And the media they consume shapes how they perceive your brand. Our influencer & PR strategies help you:

  • ✔ Build social proof and brand authority
  • ✔ Reach hyper-targeted communities through trusted voices
  • ✔ Strengthen reputation across digital and traditional media
  • ✔ Launch products or campaigns with impact and reach
  • ✔ Drive website traffic, engagement, and conversions organically

TwicTech helps your story reach the right people through the right platforms — with the right strategy.
